I Need Feedback On Stuff

Publish a note asking people for feedback on whatever you're working on, and include "#infoTEXT" where you replace "TEXT" with the field relevant to what you're asking feedback on. For example: "What do you guys think of my game? #infoGamedev".

Note: This website/service is in its early OhShitItsWorkingSomehow phase. There's still more to develop (search doesn't work) like to browse notes by specific hashtags, curation of quality feedbackers based on multiple different criteria/filters, and among other things like allowing you to login with an extension like Alby, allowing you to publish notes and reply, zapping, etc. It's still under development and I'm not confident that I'll be able to finish by reaching the desired state, with that said, you're more than welcome to message me if you're interested in co-developing it (I'm more of a front-end dev and design guy).
